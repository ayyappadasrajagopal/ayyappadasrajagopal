PhD · Electrical Eng.

Hello, I'm Dr. Ayyappadas Rajagopal

I hold a PhD in Electrical Engineering from IIT Palakkad, India. My doctoral research focused on optimal state estimation and control for networked systems under uncertain and unreliable communication channels.

I am passionate about bridging rigorous mathematical theory with real-world engineering — from designing resilient control algorithms to building hardware-in-the-loop testbeds that validate theoretical models in real time.

Currently, I am co-founding a startup focused on networked control systems and robotics, bringing academia-quality innovation into industry applications. I'm also preparing manuscripts for top-tier journals and patent filings.
